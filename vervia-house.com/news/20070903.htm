Vervia’s wedding registry and Zen Hearts collection
add romantic flair to the Autumn 2007 ZEXY
Bridal Fiesta exhibition

Shanghai 03 September 2007.

Vervia adds romantic flair to weddings in Shanghai by presenting the Vervia Wedding Gift Registry and the precious Zen Hearts design collection on the Autumn 2007 ZEXY Bridal Fiesta exhibition. In the run-up to the popular wedding season of October, the exhibition will take place on the 8-9th September at the Shanghai Mart. and is expected to attract at least 10,000 people who intend to get married.

The precious ‘Zen hearts’ collection has been specially created to celebrate special romantic moments. The blissful range includes champagne glasses, shot glasses, plates, glass lanterns - all engraved with our romantic, delicate Zen hearts emblem, reflecting two hearts as one C joined in perfect unison. Zen Hearts is a reflection upon ancient Chinese calligraphy, the Zen buddhist enso symbol of enlightenment, and the Taoist symbol of the masculine and feminine energy in all things.

For those who wish for more personal, individual gifts for their wedding - rather than impersonal cash envelopes, Vervia has also launched the Vervia Wedding Registry service. This brilliant gift registry provides a one-stop service, allowing couples to register for the gifts they wish, then informing their friends and family, who can then conveniently purchase all gifts in one location. For friends and family this is a completely new and stress-free way to purchase the right wedding gifts, which often can be a very time consuming and complicated endeavor.

Alongside the Zen hearts collection, Vervia has a wide range of unique in-house designed pieces with everything a newly wedded couple may need to start a home and family C furniture, houseware, tableware, glassware, artworks, jewellery and almost anything else you can dream of.

Vervia is an innovative lifestyle design house focusing on the organic, natural and spiritual elements of life. Vervia offers a totally fresh lifestyle vision for today and the future. While mixing visual concepts from ancient cultures with contemporary ideas, Vervia tries to bring a vivid energy and natural balance to people’s homes and life.
