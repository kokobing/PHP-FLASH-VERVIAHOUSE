Vervia kicks off with Vervia Weekend Specials!
July 07-2007

Vervia launches Vervia Weekend Specials to add more buzz and flavour to the already sizzling bohemian enclave in Taikang Lu. Every Saturday from 16:00-18:00 a special guest will bring their own innovations to the table C be it by mixing creative drinks or by preparing special food and snacks. ‘Everyone is welcome. We want to invite the public to experiment, innovate and share their very own “specials” with the wider Vervia community’, says Iris Hreinsdottir from the Vervia design team.’ A string of mysterious strangers are scheduled to make special guest performances: Grungy Grimm, Magic Maggie, Tantalizing Tomas, Creole Coleman, Krazee Kim, Lightning Lizzie and Juicy Javith promise to surprise your taste buds - with their English accent, Hunan spice, Korean surprise, wild Indian mix or Latin touch and more to come…

Finally, the unique recipes created during the Vervia Weekend Specials events will be collected and published in a book, from which the proceeds will go to a local charity. Vervia also invites friends, members of the public and anyone else who wants to take part in the events to experiment and share their own special recipes. The aim is to create a new forum for ideas and bring more people together at Vervia.

Vervia is an innovative lifestyle design house focusing on the organic, natural and spiritual elements of life. Vervia offers a totally fresh lifestyle vision for today and the future. While mixing visual concepts from ancient cultures with contemporary ideas, Vervia strives to bring a vivid energy and natural balance to people’s homes and life.

Vervia Weekend Specials can be enjoyed every Saturday, from 16:00-18:00 at Vervia, shop 46, in Taikang Lu, Lane 248.

Please contact Vervia’s creative communications team for more information.
