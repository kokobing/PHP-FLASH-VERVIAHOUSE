Shanghai, 27 th January 2007.

Vervia C the house of innovation, opens its first retail shop in Shanghai

Vervia celebrates the grand opening of its first retail shop in Taikang Road, Shanghai. The shop is the first among a series of unique lifestyle design ware shops, scheduled to open later this year.

Vervia is among the very few design ware stores to have its own team of in-house designers, working exclusively on innovative designs and product development. Vervia features glassware, house ware, hand-made furniture, soft furnishings, jewelry, oleographs and their own line of trendy fashion. Joe Phat, Konkrete, Red Star, Elemental and ArTea are all brands with a special edge - designed by Vervia. While also providing tailor-made design solutions, Vervia can print patterns on any material, should a customer so desire. All Vervia products are limited editions with a ‘Zen Hearts’ collection, designed specifically for Valentine’s day.

As a full service design company Vervia engages in a wide range of activities beyond ordinary retail: graphic and interior design, tailor-made design solutions, export to international markets. Further services in planning include a wedding catalogue, a workshop for jewelry and an online shop where customers can put together their own designs.

Vervia will increase its presence by opening further outlets in Beijing, Hong Kong and another two shops in Shanghai one on Taikang Road high street, the other on Yangshu Pu Road - currently under construction by the famous Taiwanese architect Deng Kun Yan. Vervia is also negotiating a franchise shop in Holland and Korea.

Vervia ultimately strives to make life at home more beautiful and improve people’s quality of life. Situated in the artistic area of Taikang Road, the shop enjoys a cosy atmosphere with a coffee lounge, world music and a rare selection of Chinese beverages. Vervia aims to bring together the ideas of creative people around the world and forge stronger links between art, industry and the community they operate in.

The international team of designers from Switzerland, Iceland, Italy, China work around the clock to make their aesthetic vision come true. Vervia founder, Iris Hreinsdottir says: ‘We bring people a completely new shopping experience C it’s more like an adventure where they themselves can discover and - to some extent - be part of innovating the products they want. In Vervialand, they can escape from the stress of everyday life. We want people to explore Vervia, get inspired and feel refreshed.’

2007 年 1 月 27 日 上海

唯雅 C 创新之家，在上海开设第一家分店

今天，唯雅时尚家居店开业典礼在上海泰康路举行，这是唯雅系列分店中的一家，他们今年内将在北京、上海等地开设其它多家分店。

唯雅是少数即开设零售店，又拥有自己设计团队的手工艺产品设计公司，他们拥有自己独特的产品设计与发展理念。她的产品系列涵盖了玻璃器具、室内装饰、手工家具、软饰品、珠宝挂件、石版画等时尚产品领域。唯雅的乔百特、康克雷特、红星、四大要素与爱尔茶等系列，均具有与众不同的特质。在为顾客提供量身定做的设计方案的同时，唯雅可以根据顾客的特殊要求，在产品上印上自己喜欢的样式。除此之外，唯雅最近还专为情人节打造一系列“禅之心”的作品。

作为一个提供全方位服务的设计公司，唯雅还参与了一系列普通零售之外的事项：平面与室内设计、量身定做的设计方案等等。将要开设的服务，包括婚礼礼品系列设计、开设珠宝设计课程，以及建设给客户展示才能的网上平台。

唯雅还将进军北京、香港等地，在上海的另外两家分店也在紧锣密鼓地筹划当中――将设在泰康路主街道与杨树浦路上――由台湾著名室内建筑设计师登琨艳主持建造。除此之外，唯雅在波兰与韩国的分店也在筹划之中。

唯雅最终目标，是为了给人们的家居生活创造更多的美好、惬意元素。坐落于富有艺术氛围的泰康路的唯雅第一家分店，内设一间环境优雅的休闲吧，顾客可以在此尽情地享受中国稀有美酒及世界各地的音乐。唯雅旨在融合世界设计精英思想的精华，铸造连接艺术、行业与社会之桥梁。

唯雅国际性的设计师团队，来自包括瑞士、冰岛、意大利、中国等国家。唯雅的创始人爱丽丝说：“我们给人们带来一种完全不一样的购买体验 C 他们就像是在探险似的 C 从某种程度上讲 C 成为产品设计过程中的一部分。在唯雅坊，借一丝蝉意，他们可以短暂地逃离于繁重的工作。我们需要人们来发现唯雅，获得灵感与放松身心。”
