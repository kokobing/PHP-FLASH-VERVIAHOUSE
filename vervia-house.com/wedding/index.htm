Wedding gift registry - How it works!

唯雅新婚礼物注册 - 步骤

     
 

1. Register your contact details and the date of the wedding (online or in the shop)

1. 在唯雅婚礼网站或唯雅店内留下您的联系方式和 / 或结婚之日。

2. Select the gifts you wish your wedding guests to buy for you

2. 在唯雅店内挑选您期待从亲朋好友处收到的礼物。

     
 

3. Register your selection with Vervia

3. 将您挑选的礼物清单交给唯雅。

     
 

4. Inform your friends about the Vervia wedding gift registry and your gift selection (include the information in your wedding invitation, send an email to friends or send a Vervia flyer)

4. 通过您的结婚请柬、邮件或者通过唯雅，通知您的朋友您在唯雅的新婚礼物登记选项。

     
 

5. Friends, family and other invited wedding guests then buy gifts according to your selection at Vervia

5. 您的亲朋好友收到消息后，来唯雅店内看礼品，根据您的礼品选择清单购买礼品。

     
 

6. Vervia will package, personalise and deliver your friends' gifts to the wedding, or a preferred address

6. 唯雅将礼物包装好，将附有贺卡的礼物在您大喜之日送达到您的婚礼现场或者您所指定的其他地点。

   
 

7. Vervia sends a report with an overview of who bought which gifts

7. 唯雅将发送报告给您，通知您是您哪位朋友选择了哪种礼物。
